Bio

> I'm a 3rd year Ph.D. student at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Ribeiro. I am broadly interested in machine learning, signal processing and optimization. I have spent the last few years developing tools for learning under constraints. If you are not entirely sure about what Constrained Learning is, and why it is relevant, I encourage you to read this.
